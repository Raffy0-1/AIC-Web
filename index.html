About AIC

The Advanced Intelligence Collective (AIC) is a premier research organization dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence through interdisciplinary collaboration and cutting-edge innovation.

  • Founded in 2010 by leading AI researchers from top institutions
  • Multidisciplinary team of computer scientists, neuroscientists, and engineers
  • Focus on both fundamental research and practical applications
  • Strong partnerships with academia and industry leaders
